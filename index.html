What is the most popular taco in the world?

The most popular taco in the world is the Al Pastor taco. It is a taco that is made with pork that is marinated in pineapple juice and spices. It is then grilled on a vertical spit. The pork is then sliced and served on a corn tortilla with pineapple and onions.
